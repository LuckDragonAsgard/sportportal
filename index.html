How it works
Three steps to run your next carnival
1

Upload students once

Paste your class list (name, year level, house). Every carnival, every season, every year — students auto-roll.

2

Draw heats, pick events

One click to auto-draw heats by age and gender. Configure your events, houses, and points system for athletics, swimming or cross country.

3

Go live on carnival day

Timers on phones scan a QR to join. Results feed straight back to the scoreboard. Publish live to parents and to your district page.
