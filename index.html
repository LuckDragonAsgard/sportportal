Student data privacy
Built so schools and parents can trust it

We know student data is the sensitive piece. Here's exactly how it's protected — before you upload a single name.

🔒

Encrypted everywhere

TLS 1.3 on every connection. Student records encrypted at rest with AES-256. No data ever travels or sits in plain text.

🙈

First name + initial only, publicly

Parents and other schools only ever see a student's first name and last-name initial — the standard convention used by School Sport bodies. Full names visible only to staff signed into your school.

🎂

Birthdays never shown

Date of birth is used once, to work out age groups. It's never displayed on any page, public or private. You can even provide just the birth year if you prefer.

🇦🇺

Hosted close to Australia

Sites served from Sydney edge. Student data in Google Cloud Singapore (asia-southeast1) — the nearest region available, under Standard Contractual Clauses aligned with the Australian Privacy Principles. AU-region migration is on the roadmap for 2027.

📤

You own the data

Export everything as CSV any time. Delete everything any time. Your school has final say — we just host it for you. If you ever leave, you take your records with you.

🚫

Never sold or shared

Student data is not sold, rented, used for advertising, or shared with any third party. Ever. Not now, not after a funding round, not if we're acquired. It's in our Terms.
